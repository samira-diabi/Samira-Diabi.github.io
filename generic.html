About Me

Introduction

Hello! My name is Samira, and I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Data Analytics and Management at Carnegie Mellon University's Heinz College, with an expected graduation date in May 2024. Fun fact: I celebrate my birthday every four years because I was born on a Leap Year!

Background

Before my academic journey, I earned a Bachelor's in Petroleum Engineering from the University of Houston (Go Coogs!). Starting as a field engineer in Oil and Gas, I soon realized my passion for data and analytics. Transitioning to roles at Apple and Google in 2020, I contributed to exciting projects like developing Apple Maps' cycling feature and Google Assistant tablets in France and Canada. Now at CMU, I'm delving deeper into analytics with coursework covering SQL, Python, Tableau, and more. Last summer, I interned as an Analyst at Chegg, where I led a project to improve customer service satisfaction. My work involved creating a Key Driver Analysis survey to understand what factors affect customer satisfaction, analyzing survey data, and presenting findings to stakeholders. It was a rewarding experience!

Interests and Hobbies

Outside of work and school, I love exploring new cultures through food, staying active with sports like soccer, and diving into captivating TV shows like "The Blacklist" and "Game of Thrones." In the next two years, I plan to travel to Asia and South America and pick up tennis!

Career Goals

My career goal is to become a skilled and impactful Data Analyst. I'm passionate about using data to make informed decisions and empower organizations. I'm committed to continuously expanding my skills and knowledge to make a positive impact in the data-driven world.
